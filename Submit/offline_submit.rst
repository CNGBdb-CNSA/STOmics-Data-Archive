Offline template submission
===========================

There are some information to submitted with offline template:

- Sample
- Tissue Section
- Experiment & Run (if sequencing reads choose to be submitted)
- STOmics Analysis
- Other

These templates can be downloaded at :download:`https://ftp.cngb.org/pub/stomics/`.

Character limitation
---------------------

These templates are restricted to be filled in English. The following special characters can be used.

- The Greek alphabet

+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| Letter  | Symbol  | Letter  | Symbol  | Letter  | Symbol  |
+=========+=========+=========+=========+=========+=========+
| alpha   | α       |iota     | ι       | rho     | ρ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| beta    | β       | kappa   | κ       | sigma   | σ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| gamma   | γ       | lambda  | λ       | tau     | τ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| delta   | δ       | mu      | μ       | upsilon | υ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| epsilon | ε       | nu      | ν       | phi     | φ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| zeta    | ζ       | xi      | ξ       | chi     | χ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| eta     | η       | omicron | ο       | psi     | ψ       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+
| theta   | θ       | pi      | π       | omega   | ω       |
+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+---------+

- Special characters

  | Temperature symbol: °
  | Plus/minus sign: ±
  | Multiplication sign: ×
